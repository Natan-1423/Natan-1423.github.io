Rules

Natan is a friendly and welcoming community. We welcome everyone to help maintain a constructive space by following the guidelines below.

  • Treat everyone with respect: Do not insult, abuse, or threaten other users, off-site or on-site.
  • Be safe: Keep personal and contact information private.
  • Be honest: Do not impersonate others or intentionally spread disinformation.
  • Try not to break the site: Report any security issues.
  • Have fun: Keep it a fun and safe place for everyone.
