Rules

Natan is a friendly and welcoming community. We welcome everyone to help maintain a constructive space by following the guidelines below.

  1. Treat everyone with respect: Do not insult, abuse, or threaten other users, off-site or on-site.
  2. Be safe: Keep personal and contact information private; do not share sensitive information such as your physical address.
  3. Be honest: Do not impersonate others or intentionally spread disinformation.
  4. Try not to break the site: If you find any security issues, please stop before causing any damage and report issues to us.
  5. Have fun: I hope you enjoy your time on Natan; please try to keep it a fun and safe place for everyone.
